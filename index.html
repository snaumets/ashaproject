Project Title

Project focus

The focus of this project is back up coaching to children from Slums and women empowerment.

RTE/Sustainability

  • Back Up Coaching to students who are already going to formal schools.
  • Vocational training for self-reliance.
  • Software training and in-house employment opportunities like call centers.

Ongoing Work

  • Education for children in slums
  • Women empowerment in communities to avoid teenage marriages

Strength

  • Providing 200+ student coaching support with less than $4 a month per child
  • Enrollment in school increase from 20% to 50% in 5 years.
  • Girls' age of marriage shifted to 18-19 from 12-13 and 5 Trinita girls are in Class 12.

Challenges

  • First generation learning with parents forcing to drop out if grades are not good.
  • Many are forced to work as domestic servants to help supplement family income
